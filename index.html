This is VoteCupertino, a website designed to bring the voting polls to you! If you enter your address, you'll receive the breakdown of voting centers and their distances to your home.

Voting can sometimes be a daunting task; this web application aims to make the process more accessible, and help people focus simply on voting. By making the process to find a voting center easier, this initiative hopes to encourage more people to vote, thereby increasing overall voter turnout for Cupertino residents. Let's make our voices heard in this year's election!
